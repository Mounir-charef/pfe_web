Hybrid approach using fuzzy sets and extreme learning machine for classifying clinical datasets


Article

9 January 2016 Anna University

Kindie Biredagn Nahato    Khanna H. Nehemiah    A. Kannan


voir article
  • follow
  • recommend
  • share


Titre Description:

Data mining techniques play a major role in developing computer aided diagnosis systems and expert systems that will aid a physician in clinical decision making. In this work, a classifier that combines the relative merits of fuzzy sets and extreme learning machine (FELM) for clinical datasets is proposed.
